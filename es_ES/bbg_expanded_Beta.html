Civ VI BBG Beta Expanded Leaders Description


Galia Vercingetorix

Cultura de Hallstatt


Las Minas dan una bonificación por adyacencia menor para todos los distritos y reciben +1 a Cultura al investigar la tecnología Forja de bronce. Los distritos especializados no reciben una adyacencia menor por estar adyacentes a otro distrito y no se pueden construir adyacentes al Centro urbano.
• Sesgo: T3 Hierro, T4 Recursos de Cantera, T5 Recursos Minables.


Gallic Confederation


+1 Culture on mines with Bronze Working.

+1 Influence per turn per Workshop and Recruiting a Great Engineer grants 1 envoy.


Oppidum

Un distrito exclusivo de Galia que es más barato y está disponible antes que el distrito al que sustituye, la Zona industrial. El distrito de Oppidum es defendible con ataques a distancia.

+2 de bonificación por adyacencia a la Producción por Canteras y recursos estratégicos. Provoca un Bombardeo cultural en territorio neutral.


Gaesatae

Unidad exclusiva gala de la Antigüedad que sustituye al Guerrero. Esta unidad tiene más coste y recibe +10 a la Fuerza de combate cuando luche contra unidades que tengan más Fuerza de combate básica. +5 a la Fuerza de combate contra defensas de distritos.


Macedonia Olympias

Fusión helenística


Al conquistar una ciudad recibe +20% a la Producción en todas las ciudades por 10 turnos, una Eureka por cada Campamento o Campus en la ciudad conquistada y una Inspiración por cada Lugar sagrado o Teatro.
• Sesgo: Ninguno.


Cabeiri Mysteries


Cities with a Basilikoi and Holy Site give 0.5 science per population. +1 envoy when a city first has both a Basilikoi and Holy Site. +2 Combat Strength when attacking for melee and heavy cavalry units per City-state you are Suzerain of.


Basilikoi Paides

Un edificio exclusivo de Macedonia. +25 % de experiencia de combate para todas las unidades terrestres cuerpo a cuerpo, a distancia y Hetairoi entrenadas en esta ciudad. Obtiene Ciencia equivalente al 20 % del costo de Producción de de la unidad cuando una unidad no civil se entrena en esta ciudad.

Las eservas de Recursos Estratégicos aumentaron +10 (a velocidad estándar).

No se puede construir en un distrito de Campamento que ya tenga un Establo.

+2 Hierro por turno una vez descubierto.


Hipaspista

Unidad Cuerpo a cuerpo exclusiva de Macedonia que sustituye al Soldado con espada. +10 a la Fuerza de combate al asediar distritos. +50% de bonificación de apoyo adicional.

Forja del hierro Tecnología


Maya Te' K'inich II

Mayab


Asentarse adyacente a Costa o Agua Dulce no da Alojamiento adicional. El Palacio recibe +1 Alojamiento . Las Granjas otorgan +1 Alojamiento adicional, +1 Producción por cada Observatorio adyacente y +1 Oro . Las Plantaciones otorgan +1 Ciencia por cada Observatorio adyacente tras investigar Educación. +1 Servicios por cada Recurso de Lujo adyacente al Centro de la Ciudad. +3 Fuerza de Combate a unidades a 6 casillas de tu capital.

• Sesgo: T3 Lujo de Plantaciones, T4 Plátanos. Especial "Hidrofóbico".


Wrath of Venus


Once you enter the classical era, receive -2 combat strength for all units within six tiles of your capital. Units beyond 6 tiles receive +4 combat strength. Units heal 5hp and gain 2 experience points upon completing an eureka.


Observatorio

Un distrito exclusivo de los mayas y destinado a fines científicos. Sustituye al distrito de Campus y es más barato de construir.

+1 Ciencia por cada Plantación adyacente. +1 por cada dos Grangas o casillas de destrito adyacentes.


Hul'che

Unidad de ataque a distancia de la Antigüedad exclusiva de los mayas. +5 a la Fuerza de combate al luchar contra un oponente herido.

Tiro con arco Tecnología


Fenicia Ahiram

Colonias mediterráneas


Comienza el juego con la tecnología Escritura Eureka. Las ciudades costeras fundadas por Fenicia y ubicadas en el mismo continente que la Capital fenicia son 100% leales. Los colonos ganan +2 Movimiento y +2 rango de visión mientras están embarcados. Los colonos ignoran los costos adicionales de Movimiento por embarcarse y desembarcarse.
• Sesgo: T1 Costa.


Merchant Princes


International trade routes provides +0.5 Production and +2 Gold for each Luxury resource in the origin city.

Unlock the Royal Tomb unique building.


Cothon

Un distrito exclusivo de Fenicia que sustituye al Puerto y es más barato de construir. Debe construirse en terreno de Costa o Lago adyacente a tierra.

+25% a la Producción dirigida a construir unidades Navales y Colonos en esta ciudad.


Royal Tomb

Replaces the Library. +1 Science per Great Admiral recruited by Ahiram after this city was founded or conquered.
Doesn't cost maintenance.


Birreme

Unidad exclusiva fenicia de la Antigüedad que sustituye a la Galera. Tiene más Fuerza de combate y Movimiento . El Birreme evita que los Comerciantes en un radio de 4 casillas sean saqueados si están en casillas de agua.

Navegación a vela Tecnología


Swahili Al-Hasan ibn Sulaiman

Coral Construction


Coastal cities gain +25% Production toward districts.

+1 Production for unimproved reef tiles with Sailing.

Bias: T1 Coast.


Palace of Husuni Kubwa


Trade Route capacity +1 for each Harbor adjacent to a World Wonder.


Pillar Tomb

Replaces the Monument.

+1 Culture,
+1 Loyalty. If Loyalty is already at maximum, +1 Culture.
+2 Gold and +1 Science for outgoing International Trade Routes for every 2 districts within 2 tiles of the City Center (maximum 6 districts).


Jahazi

Swahili unique ancient era support naval unit can set up a fishing boat, which will provide +2 Gold (2 Charges). Units embarked within 2 tiles gain 1 Movement Point and ignore terrain costs. Can be captured.

Navegación a vela Tecnología


Teotihuacán Spearthrower Owl

Where Men Become Gods


When you are Suzerain of a City-state, all Trade Routes receive additional yields based on the type of City-state. If you are suzerain to 2 or more City-states of that type, triple these yields.
+1 Food for Religious
+1 Production for Millitaristic and Industrial
+3 Gold for Commercial
+1 Science for Scientific
+1 Culture for Cultural.
• Bias: None.


Lord of the West


+1 Trade route capacity when completing the Pochteca Enclave and the Chancery. Unlock the unique Governor, Fire is Born.


Pochteca Enclave

Unlocked at State Workforce and replaces the Diplomatic Quarter.
+1 Envoy when built next to a city center.
Grants a Governor Title when first completed.

Does not count towards district limit based on population and does not provide yields on internal trade routes.


Ehuatl Wearer

Unique Swordsman unlocked at Political Philosophy, +1 sight range and does not require strategic resources.

Filosofía política Principio


Thule Kiviuq

Hunting of the Bowhead


Builders can consume one Charge and one Citizen to create a Whales tile.

Tundra tiles adjacent to a Hunter's House receive +1 Food (doubled for snow). Adjacent Camps and Fishing boats receive +1 Food and +1 Production. A tile may only benefit from a single Hunter's House.

Bias: T1 Tundra, T2 Coast.


Heart Not Stone


+5 Combat Strength and +1 Movement for Recon units starting their turn on Tundra or Snow tiles.


Hunter's House

Unique improvement for Thule, unlocked at Sailing.
+1 Housing
+1 Food if built on a Tundra tile (doubled on snow)
+1 Culture and +1 Faith for each adjacent sea resource and Camp tile after Foreign Trade.

Must be built adjacent to a resource tile. Can be placed on forests. Cannot be placed next to another Hunter's House.


Dogsled Hunter

Thule unique Ancient era ranged Recon unit, replaces the Scout.

Has 1 Charge to establish a Hunter's House.


Tibet Trisong Detsen

Roof of the World


Citizens may work mountain tiles. Mountain tiles gain additional yields for each district within 2 tiles:
+2 Food per City Center,
+1 Production per Industrial Zone,
+1 Gold per Harbor and Commercial Hub,
+1 Science per Campus,
+1 Culture per Theater Square,
+1 Faith +1 per Holy Site.

Can purchase buildings in specialty districts adjacent to a mountain tile with Faith, excluding the Government Plaza district.

+5% Faith in cities with a Governor for each promotion it has.
Bias: T3 Mountains (plain, grassland, and tundra).


Capture of Chang'an


Land military units trained in a city with an Encampment and a Worship Building start with a Promotion.


Dzong

A district unique to Tibet that replaces the Holy Site district and is cheaper to build.

Occupying units receives +5 Defensive Combat Strength.
Buildings in the Dzong are 10% cheaper to purchase with Gold and Faith.


Rta Pa

Tibet unique Renaissance era ranged cavalry unit unlocked at Astronomy.

Has a ranged attack with 1 Range, can move after attacking.

Astronomía Tecnología


Fire is Born


Arrival of Strangers

May be assigned to a City-State, where it provides 2 Envoys. Friendly units inside this City-State borders gain +5 Combat strength.


Waka Outpost

Envoys sent to this City-state count as two Envoys. When established in any city or City-state, envoys sent to City-states count as two if you have an active Trade Route to that City-state.
+25% Production for Commercial Hubs and Harbors in your cities within a 15-tile radius.


Petén Expedition

+10 Combat Strength for this City-state's garrison.


Fall of Jaguar Paw

+25% Production for Commercial Hub, Harbor, and City Center buildings in your cities within a 15-tile radius.


Assault on Uaxactun

Levied units within 10 tiles of this City-state receive +5 Combat Strength.


Overseer of Kings

+2 Diplomatic Favor per City-state you are Suzerain of.
