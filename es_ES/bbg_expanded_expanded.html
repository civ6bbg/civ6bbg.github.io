Galia Vercingetorix

Cultura de Hallstatt


Las Minas dan una bonificación por adyacencia menor para todos los distritos y reciben +1 a Cultura al investigar la tecnología Forja de bronce. Los distritos especializados no reciben una adyacencia menor por estar adyacentes a otro distrito y no se pueden construir adyacentes al Centro urbano.
• Sesgo: T3 Hierro, T4 Recursos de Cantera, T5 Recursos Minables.


Gallic Confederation


Your units receive +1 Combat Strength for each type of City-State you are Suzerain of. Receive a free Envoy whenever you recruit a Great General. When an Encampment or Oppidum is built, receive Ancient Walls in that City.


Oppidum

Un distrito exclusivo de Galia que es más barato y está disponible antes que el distrito al que sustituye, la Zona industrial. El distrito de Oppidum es defendible con ataques a distancia.

+2 de bonificación por adyacencia a la Producción por Canteras y recursos estratégicos. Provoca un Bombardeo cultural en territorio neutral.


Gaesatae

Unidad exclusiva gala de la Antigüedad que sustituye al Guerrero. Esta unidad tiene más coste y recibe +10 a la Fuerza de combate cuando luche contra unidades que tengan más Fuerza de combate básica. +5 a la Fuerza de combate contra defensas de distritos.


Teotihuacán Spearthrower Owl

Where Men Become Gods


May not build Settlers. All Trade Routes grant +1 Envoy when sent to a City-state. Type bonuses from City-States following your Religion are increased by 100%.


Lord of the West


Has access to the Emissary Unique Governor, Fire Is Born. The Capital gains +1 Population when a City-State is Conquered.


Pochteca Enclave

Replaces the Diplomatic Quarter. Unlocked at State Workforce. Provides +1 Trade Route capacity, with an additonal +1 per adjacent District, and +2 per adjacent Wonder. Grants a major adjacency bonus to adjacent specialty Districts. Generates +1 Great Prophet Points.


Ehuatl Wearer

Replaces the Swordsmen. Unlocked at Early Empire. Does not require Iron. When at least one is within 3 tiles of a City-State, Envoys sent to that City-State are doubled.

Se desbloquea con Imperio inicial Principio


Thule Kiviuq

Hunting of the Bowhead


Workers may build a Whales luxury resource at the cost of 1 population.


A Heart Not Made of Stone


All Cities generate +10% Faith for every Reconnaissance unit with at least 1 Promotion. Reconnaissance units gain +1 Movement and +10 Combat Strength while on Tundra, Snow, Tundra Hills, or Snow Hills terrain.


Whalebone House

Unlocked at Celestial Navigation. Must be placed adjacent to 3 Coast tiles. Provides +1 Food, and +1 Production to each adjacent Fishing Boats improvement. Provides +1 Culture, +1 Faith, and +1 Tourism for each adjacent Sea Resource. +1 Housing.


Dogsled Raider

Skirmisher Replacement. Unlocked at Construction. Much cheaper. -5 Combat Strength when defending. +10 Combat Strength when attacking units at full health.

Se desbloquea con Construcción Tecnología


Macedonia Olympias

Fusión helenística


Al conquistar una ciudad recibe +20% a la Producción en todas las ciudades por 10 turnos, una Eureka por cada Campamento o Campus en la ciudad conquistada y una Inspiración por cada Lugar sagrado o Teatro.
• Sesgo: Ninguno.


Cabeiri Mysteries


Receives an Envoy from founding a Pantheon. Assigning Envoys to City-States grants Great General and Great Prophet Points, doubling if to Militaristic and Religious City-States.


Basilikoi Paides

Un edificio exclusivo de Macedonia. +25 % de experiencia de combate para todas las unidades terrestres cuerpo a cuerpo, a distancia y Hetairoi entrenadas en esta ciudad. Obtiene Ciencia equivalente al 20 % del costo de Producción de de la unidad cuando una unidad no civil se entrena en esta ciudad.

Las eservas de Recursos Estratégicos aumentaron +10 (a velocidad estándar).

No se puede construir en un distrito de Campamento que ya tenga un Establo.

+2 Hierro por turno una vez descubierto.


Hipaspista

Unidad Cuerpo a cuerpo exclusiva de Macedonia que sustituye al Soldado con espada. +10 a la Fuerza de combate al asediar distritos. +50% de bonificación de apoyo adicional.

Se desbloquea con Forja del hierro Tecnología


Maya Te' K'inich II

Mayab


Asentarse adyacente a Costa o Agua Dulce no da Alojamiento adicional. El Palacio recibe +1 Alojamiento . Las Granjas otorgan +1 Alojamiento adicional, +1 Producción por cada Observatorio adyacente y +1 Oro . Las Plantaciones otorgan +1 Ciencia por cada Observatorio adyacente tras investigar Educación. +1 Servicios por cada Recurso de Lujo adyacente al Centro de la Ciudad. +3 Fuerza de Combate a unidades a 6 casillas de tu capital.

• Sesgo: T3 Lujo de Plantaciones, T4 Plátanos. Especial "Hidrofóbico".


Wrath of Venus


Damaging enemy Cities provides a burst of Science equal to the damage the City received, and conquering an Original Capital grants a random Eureka. Discovering a Technology while at war instantly heals military units by +25 Health and grants them +5 Experience.


Observatorio

Un distrito exclusivo de los mayas y destinado a fines científicos. Sustituye al distrito de Campus y es más barato de construir.

+1 Ciencia por cada Plantación adyacente. +1 por cada dos Grangas o casillas de destrito adyacentes.


Hul'che

Unidad de ataque a distancia de la Antigüedad exclusiva de los mayas. +5 a la Fuerza de combate al luchar contra un oponente herido.

Se desbloquea con Tiro con arco Tecnología


Fenicia Ahiram

Colonias mediterráneas


Comienza el juego con la tecnología Escritura Eureka. Las ciudades costeras fundadas por Fenicia y ubicadas en el mismo continente que la Capital fenicia son 100% leales. Los colonos ganan +2 Movimiento y +2 rango de visión mientras están embarcados. Los colonos ignoran los costos adicionales de Movimiento por embarcarse y desembarcarse.
• Sesgo: T1 Costa.


Merchant Princes


Purchasing buildings generates a burst of Great Merchant points. Unlock the Royal Tomb unique building.


Cothon

Un distrito exclusivo de Fenicia que sustituye al Puerto y es más barato de construir. Debe construirse en terreno de Costa o Lago adyacente a tierra.

+25% a la Producción dirigida a construir unidades Navales y Colonos en esta ciudad.


Royal Tomb

Replaces the Library. Provides bonus Gold equal to the adjacency Science bonus of its district. +1 Science for each Great Merchant earnt by this civilization.


Birreme

Unidad exclusiva fenicia de la Antigüedad que sustituye a la Galera. Tiene más Fuerza de combate y Movimiento . El Birreme evita que los Comerciantes en un radio de 4 casillas sean saqueados si están en casillas de agua.

Se desbloquea con Navegación a vela Tecnología


Swahili Al-Hasan ibn Sulaiman

Coral Construction


Districts in coastal Cities receive a Major adjacency bonus from the City Center, and a standard bonus from being adjacent to a Harbor. +2 Production from Reefs.


Palace of Husuni Kubwa


Harbours adjacent to a Wonder provide +1 Trade Route Capacity. Patronage of Great People costs 20% less Gold.


Pillar Tomb

A building unique to the Swahili. It is cheaper than the Monument which it replaces. +1 Culture, +1 Loyalty. International Trade Routes from this city generate +1 Gold, +1 Culture for each of this city’s districts that are within 2 tiles of the City Centre.


Jahazi

Naval support unit, unique to the Swahili. Embarked units within 2 tiles ignore terrain costs and receive +1 Movement. Has 2 charges to build Fishing Boats. Fishing Boats built by a Jahazi yield +1 Production, +2 Gold.

Se desbloquea con Navegación a vela Tecnología


Tibet Trisong Detsen

Roof of the World


Citizens may work Mountain tiles. Mountain tiles receive yields from districts within 2 tiles: +2 Food from City Centres, and +1 of the appropriate yield from other districts. May purchase buildings with Faith in specialty districts next to Mountains. Cities with an established Governor receive +5% Faith for each promotion that Governor has.


Capture of Chang’an


+1 Great General points in cities with both an Encampment and a Dzong. Land combat units in those cities begin with a free promotion if the city has a Worship Building.


Dzong

A district unique to Tibet for religious activity. Replaces the Holy Site district and cheaper to build.

Awards +1 Governor Title when built adjacent to at least 4 Mountains. Buildings in the Dzong district are 20% cheaper to purchase with Gold and Faith.

Occupying unit receives +10 Defense Strength. Must be built on a Hill.


Rta Pa

Tibetan unique Renaissance era ranged cavalry unit. Has a a Range of 1, but can move after attacking. No Movement penalty in hill terrain. Unlocks with Astronomy.

Se desbloquea con Astronomía Tecnología


Fire Is Born


Arrival of Strangers

May be assigned to a City-State. Friendly Units defending this city gain +5 Combat Strength.


Waka Outpost

While established in a City-State, provides a copy of its Luxury resources.


Petén Expedition

City-State garrisons within 9 tiles lose -5 Combat Strength. This city garrison gains +5 Combat Strength.


Fall of Jaguar Paw

Friendly Spies in this city act as if 3 levels more experienced when Siphoning Funds or Fabricating Scandals, and complete these missions 25% faster.


Assault on Uaxactun

Conquered Cities within 9 tiles gain +25% Production towards City Center and Commercial Hub buildings.


Overseer of Kings

Vassal City-States within 9 tiles generate +1 Diplomatic Favor. Conquered Cities within 9 tiles permanently generate +1 Diplomatic Favor.
