Civ VI BBG 6.5 World Congress


Arms Control

All players have their Weapons of Mass Destruction set equal to the target player's.

The target player loses all of their Weapons of Mass Destruction.

Earliest Era: Modern Era


Border Control Treaty

Tiles bought by this player are 50% cheaper.

This player's borders will not grow via Culture.

Latest Era: Modern Era


Deforestation Treaty

Prohibits chopping or clearing Features of the chosen type.

Clearing Features of the chosen type yields Gold equal to the Production and Food.

Earliest Era: Industrial Era

Latest Era: Information Era


Espionage Pact

Spies executing the chosen Operation function 2 levels higher.

The chosen Operation is unavailable.

Earliest Era: Renaissance Era

Latest Era: Atomic Era


Global Energy Treaty

Ban the production of buildings of this type.

50% discount on the production of buildings of this type.

Earliest Era: Renaissance Era


Heritage Organization

Tourism from Great Works of this type is doubled.

No Tourism from Great Works of this type.

Earliest Era: Industrial Era


Luxury Policy

Duplicates of the chosen Luxury resource grant Amenities.

The chosen Luxury resource grants no Amenities.


Mercenary Companies

The cost to produce or purchase military units using the chosen currency type is increased by 50% until the next World Congress.

The cost to produce or purchase military units using the chosen currency type is reduced by 25% until the next World Congress.


Migration Treaty

+20% faster Population growth but -5 Loyalty per turn in this player's cities.

+5 Loyalty per turn but -20% Population growth in this player's cities.

Earliest Era: Medieval Era


Military Advisory

Units of the chosen promotion class gain 3 Combat Strength.

Units of the chosen promotion class lose 3 Combat Strength.

Latest Era: Atomic Era


Patronage

Earn double points towards Great People of this class.

No points earned towards Great People of this class.

Latest Era: Modern Era


Public Works Program

+100% Production towards this Project.

-50% Production towards this Project.

Earliest Era: Industrial Era

Latest Era: Information Era


Sovereignty

+100% of the City-States' yield type when sending Trade Routes to a City-State of this type.

City-States of this type do not provide their unique Suzerain bonus.

Latest Era: Modern Era


Trade Policy

International Trade Routes sent to the chosen player provide +4 Gold to the sender. The chosen player receives +1 Trade Route capacity.

International Trade Routes sent to the chosen player or from the chosen player provide -4 Gold (can be negative).


Urban Development Treaty

+100% Production towards buildings in this district.

No buildings can be created in this district.

Latest Era: Modern Era


World Ideology

This Government type gains a Wildcard policy slot.

This Government type loses a Wildcard policy slot.

Earliest Era: Medieval Era


World Religion

+10 Religious Strength for all religious units of this Religion.

All players may condemn units of the chosen Religion, and doing so grants 25 Diplomatic Favor.

Latest Era: Industrial Era
