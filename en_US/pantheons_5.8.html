Dance of the Aurora

Holy Site districts get +1 Faith from adjacent flat Tundra tiles.

Desert Folklore

Holy Site districts get +1 Faith from adjacent Desert tiles.

Sacred Path

Holy Site districts get +1 Faith from adjacent Rainforest and Woods tiles.

River Goddess

Rivers give +1 Faith adjacency to Holy Site Districts. +1 Amenities and +1 Housing to cities if they have a Holy Site district adjacent to a River.

Monument to the Gods

+20% Production towards wonders.

Divine Spark

+1 Great Person point for Holy Site ( Great Prophet), Campus ( Great Scientist) and Industrial Zone ( Great Engineer) districts, Amphiteater ( Great Writer) building.

Lady of the Reeds and Marshes

+1 Production from Marsh, Oasis, and Floodplains.

God of the Sea

+1 Production from Fishing Boats.

God of the Open Sky

+1 Culture from Pastures.

Goddess of the Hunt

+1 Food and +2 Gold from Camps.

Stone Circles

+1 Production and +1 Faith from Quarries.

Religious Idols

+3 Faith and +3 Gold from Mines over Luxury and Bonus resources.

God of Craftsmen

+1 Production and +1 Faith from improved Strategic resources.

Goddess of Festivals

+1 Culture from Plantations.

God of the Forge

+30% Production toward Ancient and Classical military units.

Initiation Rites

When chosen receive a Warrior in your Capital. Receive 30% of a non-civilian unit's production value in Faith when produced.

Goddess of Tides

+1 Culture from Fishing Boats.

God of War and Plunder

+1 Great Person point for Encampment ( Great General), Harbor ( Great Admiral), and Commerical Hub ( Great Merchant) districts.

Fertility Rites

+1 Food for Cattle, Rice, Wheat, and Maize resources.

Religious Settlements

+20% Production towards Settlers.
Border expansion rate is 50% faster and new cities get 2 free tiles on foundation.

City Patron Goddess

+40% Production toward all districts in cities without a specialty district.

Earth Goddess

+1 Faith from tiles with Charming or better Appeal.

Fire Goddess

+3 Faith from Geothermal Fissures and Volcanic Soil.
